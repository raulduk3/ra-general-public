# Project Presentation

Our Project Presentation Circle Time consists of 4 sections:Â 

1. **Scenario:**Â The "why", the hook of the project, it is the story that ties all the other parts together.Â 
2. **Subject:**Â The "what", the context about the given thing we are building to get every student to the level of understanding needed to fully enjoy the project and understand why we are building it.Â 
3. **Concepts and Terms:**Â The "how", the explanation of physics, engineering, and LEGO building words that link to our project for the day.
